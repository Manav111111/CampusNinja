Privacy Policy for Campus Ninja

Effective Date: July 1, 2026 • Last Updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Campus Ninja ("we," "our," or "us"). We are dedicated to respecting your privacy and protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our Android mobile application (Campus Ninja) and associated services.

By downloading, installing, or using Campus Ninja, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide an optimized and tailored educational experience, we collect only necessary information. The data we collect falls into the following categories:

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration Data: When you create an account using your email address or Google Sign-In, we collect your basic profile details, such as your email address, full name, and profile picture (if provided).
  • Academic Setup Preferences: Information regarding your educational institution, degree course, engineering branch, and semester selection to customize study resources.
  • User-Generated Content: Bookmarks, saved resources, study lists, or inquiries submitted through the app.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Non-personally identifiable information such as device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., push notification tokens), and preferred app language.
  • Usage Statistics: Information about how you interact with our study notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), and educational video links to help us optimize server delivery and content availability.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

  1. Core Service Delivery: To authenticate your account, maintain your session, and provide academic study materials appropriate for your branch and semester.
  2. Push Notifications: To deliver important educational updates, new study material announcements, and administrative alerts.
  3. App Improvement: To analyze technical performance, troubleshoot crashes, and improve user experience and interface responsiveness.
  4. Communication & Support: To respond to user feedback, inquiries, or technical support requests.
  5. Security & Prevention: To safeguard against unauthorized access, malicious usage, or terms violation.

4. Authentication Information

Campus Ninja utilizes Supabase Authentication for secure identity verification.

  • Standard Sign-Up: If you register via email and password, your credentials are salted and securely hashed using industry-standard cryptography before being stored on Supabase servers. We never store raw passwords.
  • Google Sign-In: If you authenticate using Google Sign-In, we receive an authentication token alongside your public Google profile information (name and email address). We do not gain access to your private Google account data, contacts, or passwords.

5. Device Information

When running on your Android device, Campus Ninja accesses minimal device telemetry strictly to ensure platform compatibility and responsive UI rendering. We do not access sensitive hardware features such as your microphone, camera, contacts list, or precise GPS background location unless explicitly authorized for a specific in-app feature.

6. Push Notifications

To keep you informed about new notes, syllabus updates, and community alerts, we use Expo Push Notifications and Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).

  • When you open the application, a unique device notification token is registered securely in our database.
  • You can manage or disable push notifications at any time through your Android device system settings (Settings > Apps > Campus Ninja > Notifications).

7. Data Storage and Security

Infrastructure Provider

Our backend cloud database, user authentication infrastructure, and file storage systems are powered by Supabase (hosted on enterprise-grade cloud servers).

Security Practices

  • All data transmissions between your Android app and our servers are encrypted using modern Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS).
  • Database access is strictly controlled via Row-Level Security (RLS) policies, ensuring that authenticated users can only modify their own account profiles.
  • While we implement stringent security measures, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% infallible.

8. Third-Party Services

We integrate limited, reputable third-party infrastructure providers to operate our application:

  • Supabase Inc.: Database, cloud storage, and user authentication infrastructure.
  • Google Cloud / Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Push notification routing.
  • Expo: Application runtime and native hardware bridging.

These third-party service providers operate under their own strict privacy policies and data protection agreements compliant with international data standards.

9. External Links

Campus Ninja provides external links to curated educational resources and community platforms, including:

  • YouTube: For embedded or external educational lectures and video tutorials.
  • WhatsApp & Instagram: For student discussion forums, community groups, and announcements.

When you click on these external links, you will leave Campus Ninja and enter third-party platforms. We do not control and are not responsible for the data collection, privacy policies, or content practices of those external websites or apps.

10. Children's Privacy

Campus Ninja is designed for college and university students. We do not knowingly solicit or collect personally identifiable data from children under the age of 13. If a parent or legal guardian discovers that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without consent, please contact us immediately at support@example.com, and we will promptly delete all associated records from our servers.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal data and account records only for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with educational services.

  • Cached study files stored locally on your mobile device remain until you clear the app cache or uninstall the application.
  • If you delete your account, your personal identification records, push notification tokens, and profile setups are permanently purged from our active databases within 30 days.

12. User Rights

Under Google Play policies and applicable data protection regulations, you retain full ownership and rights over your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a summary of the personal profile data stored in your account.
  • Right to Correction: You can update or modify your academic branch, semester, and display name directly within the application settings.
  • Right to Revoke Consent: You may revoke push notification permissions or log out of your session at any time.

13. Account Deletion Process

We provide a direct, transparent mechanism for users to permanently delete their accounts and associated data:

  1. In-App Deletion: Navigate to Profile > Settings > Delete Account within the Campus Ninja app and confirm your request.
  2. Email Deletion Request: Alternatively, you can send an account deletion request from your registered email address to support@example.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will terminate your session and permanently erase your user credentials, saved resources, and device tokens from our Supabase database.

14. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Campus Ninja does not utilize web browser cookies or cross-app advertising tracking technologies. We do not share, sell, or rent user data to data brokers, ad networks, or third-party marketing entities.

15. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect app feature enhancements, infrastructure improvements, or regulatory changes.

  • Whenever material modifications occur, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and notify users via an in-app banner or push notification.
  • Continued usage of Campus Ninja following any posted updates indicates your acknowledgment and acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
